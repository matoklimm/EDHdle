Privacy Policy

1. General Information

EDHdle is a personal, non-commercial side project.
Protecting your privacy is important to me.
This page explains what data is collected and how it is used.

In short: EDHdle does not collect, store, or process any personal data on a server.

2. No Personal Data Collection

EDHdle does not:

  • require user accounts
  • collect names, email addresses, or identifiers
  • use tracking, analytics, or advertising services
  • use cookies for tracking purposes

No personal data is transmitted to or stored on any backend server.

3. Local Storage Usage

EDHdle uses your browser’s localStorage to improve the gameplay experience.

This includes:

  • storing your guesses for the current game mode
  • remembering your progress for the daily puzzle

This data:

  • stays entirely on your device
  • is never sent to a server
  • can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser data

Local storage is required for the game to function correctly.

4. External Resources (Card Images)

Card images are loaded directly from Scryfall’s CDN (*.scryfall.io).

When images are loaded, your browser may transmit technical information such as:

  • IP address
  • browser type
  • operating system

This is standard behavior when loading external images and is governed by Scryfall’s own privacy policy. No additional data is shared by EDHdle.

5. External Links

EDHdle contains links to external websites, including:

  • EDHREC
  • Scryfall
  • GitHub
  • Buy Me a Coffee

When you follow these links, the respective provider’s privacy policy applies.
EDHdle has no control over external websites.

6. Cookies

EDHdle does not set any cookies for tracking or analytics.

Third-party websites linked from EDHdle may use cookies according to their own policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

EDHdle does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Since no personal data is collected at all, this website is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the functionality of EDHdle changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
