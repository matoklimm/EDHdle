Data Sources

EDHdle is based on the Top 100 most played Commander cards listed on EDHREC.

Only the card names and ranking positions are taken from EDHREC. EDHdle does not crawl EDHREC and does not use their API at runtime.

All card data (colors, types, oracle text, artwork, etc.) is fetched from Scryfall using a one-time offline script.

The generated data is bundled with the application. No external card APIs are called while playing the game.
The dataset used by EDHdle was generated and processed on January 8, 2026.

Rankings and card popularity may change over time as the Commander meta evolves. Therefore, I plan to regularly refresh and process the data to keep the dataset up to date. The current plan is a refresh roughly every four weeks.
